ABOUT ME



Hey there my name is Arlan, I am a freelancer based out of Jackson WY. I have been able to call the Tetons home and have had the opportunity to work within Post Production and on Commercial Set for some amazing artists and producers while continually learning and adapting to the constant challenges of Production. In the past I have worked for Teton Gravity Research and have helped out on set with various projects that have passed through the local area. During my free time, I have been pursuing web development and constantly learning more about design and functionality. I strive to continually learn from past projects and the insight of others to continually be creative and apply these skills towards future projects.

It would be a pleasure to further connect and help out in the near future.
